Clinic Story

Founded on a simple conviction

Aurea Plast began with a shared conviction between Dr. Noor Ul Ain and Dr. Nasir Rafiq: that plastic surgery and aesthetic medicine deserve the same rigour, discretion and artistry found at the world's leading clinics — delivered locally, in Lahore. What started as a small reconstructive practice has grown into a comprehensive surgical and aesthetic destination, without ever losing its boutique, patient-first approach.

Meet The Team

Meet Dr. Noor Ul Ain & Dr. Nasir Rafiq

Plastic & Reconstructive Surgeon

Dr. Noor Ul Ain

Dr. Noor Ul Ain leads all surgical procedures at Aurea Plast, with a practice spanning facial rejuvenation, breast surgery, body contouring and complex reconstructive cases.

Qualifications & Memberships
  • MBBS, FCPS (Plastic Surgery)
  • Fellowship in Reconstructive Microsurgery
  • Member, International Society of Plastic Surgeons
Aesthetic Physician

Dr. Nasir Rafiq

Dr. Nasir Rafiq oversees the aesthetic and non-surgical treatment programme, known for a refined, proportion-led approach to injectables and skin treatments.

Qualifications & Memberships
  • MBBS, Diploma in Aesthetic Medicine
  • Advanced Injectable & Laser Certification
  • Member, Aesthetic Medicine Association
